“Behind every great man is a great woman”

At The Great Women Project, we wholeheartedly believe the famous English proverb, “Behind every great man is a great woman”. Our mission is to understand, honor and celebrate the great women of the world. We uncover what makes these women and their roles ‘behind the scenes’ unique and undeniably powerful. We appreciate how they find purpose and fulfillment serving and influencing great men. We illuminate what lessons they have learned in their honorable role and what they wish they had known sooner. We respect the compromises they made in order to help their men become “great”. We showcase the motivations that kept them going when it was really tough to be supportive. And most importantly we celebrate these great women for all they’ve done for their great men, families, communities and themselves!

If you are a “great woman” or know someone in your life who is, please provide contact information here to be interviewed and honored.

We appreciate you taking the time to share your experience, knowledge and learnings with other great women as you support a great man and contribute in countless other ways.

Thank you for the pivotal role you’ve played in history and for allowing us to highlight your HERstory!
